Apenas: UOU. Não sei se foi o trailer de Star Wars, a iminência da nova temporada de GoT (que começou ontem, diga-se), mas continuamos tendo muito conteúdo para ler e seguir.

O mais interessante dessa semana é o número de financiamentos coletivos: 15 (contra os 14 que dissemos ontem no Twitter). Quase todos já seguros do financiamento, com alguns próximos ao fim. Se você está atrás de financiar algo, essa é a hora =)

Dicas Internacionais (via Twitter)

  • Helmed Horrors and Shield Guardians would be particularly nasty against invisible opponents. (SlyFlourish)
  • Pay special attention to preparing boss battles. They're not like other encounters. (SlyFlourish)
  • Evolve! Take in new experiences and ideas and nudge your DMing style in new directions. (SlyFlourish)
  • Plant seeds early on in an adventure so confrontations with boss battles aren't just straight-up fights. (SlyFlourish)
  • As you close in on the end of a campaign, ask the players what remains in their characters' arcs that have yet to be resolved. (SlyFlourish)
  • It's ok to have a planned end to a campaign. Let the players' imaginations fill in the future of their characters after it closes. (SlyFlourish)
  • DMs, for a fun twist make a villain the PCs are familiar with align with them briefly. Circumstance makes strange bedfellows. (Grand_DM)
  • Adventurers, visit the blacksmith for some Iron Spikes. You can use them to keep open (or shut) doors. (Grand_DM)
  • Adventurers, consider adding a flask of oil to your kit. (Grand_DM)
  • Expect a shit-ton of attacks to pour out of your high level fighters right away with multi-attacks and action surges. (DnDTweets)
  • Don't force the story to ensure a villain gets away but play villains smart. Let the players see how smart they are. (DnDTweets)
  • Be prepared to improvise for all three pillars: secrets for exploration; random names for NPCs; good monsters for combat. (DnDTweets)
  • Players don't fit neatly into categories or labels. Any labels or types I use are for discussion purposes only. (shawnmerwin)
